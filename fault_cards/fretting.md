## Symptoms
- Rubbing-like or rough vibration
- Possible low-amplitude repetitive wear signatures
- May coexist with looseness or poor fit

## Likely causes
- Poor lubrication
- Small-amplitude vibration
- Insufficient interference fit

## Expected vibration signatures
- Often non-unique from vibration alone
- May show increased roughness, looseness-like components, or local defect growth later

## Confirm tests
- Check fits and preload
- Inspect bore/raceway surfaces for reddish-brown or black worn particles
- Review whether the machine sees small-amplitude oscillation

## Fixes
- Use proper lubricant
- Apply preload
- Check interference fit
- Apply lubricant film to fitting surface

## Notes
Mark this as low-to-medium observability for a vibration-only assistant; strong root-cause inference usually needs inspection or maintenance context.
