## Symptoms
- Rubbing or looseness-related vibration
- Growing clearance and instability
- Secondary wear or scoring at fit surfaces

## Likely causes
- Insufficient interference or loose fit
- Insufficient sleeve tightening
- Poor shaft or housing precision

## Expected vibration signatures
- May resemble mechanical looseness with harmonics and instability
- Can coexist with bearing-defect frequencies if the bearing is already damaged

## Confirm tests
- Check shaft/housing fit and sleeve tightening
- Inspect for circumferential scoring or shiny slipped surfaces
- Use phase and harmonics to separate from pure unbalance

## Fixes
- Correct interference fit and tightening
- Improve shaft/housing precision
- Consider axial preload or anti-rotation measures where appropriate

## Notes
A useful 'don’t mislabel as only bearing damage' card because fit problems can masquerade as generic looseness.
