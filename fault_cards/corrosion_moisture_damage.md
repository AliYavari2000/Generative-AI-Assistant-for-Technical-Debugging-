# Corrosion / moisture damage

| Field | Value |
|-------|-------|
| **ID** | BRG-019 |
| **Category** | damage_mechanism |
| **Observability from Vibration** | low_medium |
| **Diagnostic Confidence** | medium_high |
| **Affected Components** | raceways, rolling_elements |
| **Aliases** | moisture-induced pitting | rust damage |

## Symptoms
- Visible rust or dark staining on bearing surfaces
- Pitting or etching on raceways from corrosive attack
- Increased vibration after prolonged standstill in humid environment

## Likely Causes
- Water or moisture ingress through failed seals
- Condensation during temperature cycling in standby equipment
- Exposure to corrosive chemicals or process fluids

## Vibration / Diagnostic Signatures
- Broadband vibration increase from surface irregularities
- Defect frequencies may emerge as corrosion pits act as stress risers for fatigue

## Confirmatory Tests
- Visual inspection for rust or etching patterns on raceway
- Lubricant water content test (Karl Fischer titration or crackle test)
- Environmental assessment of bearing operating conditions

## Recommended Fixes
- Upgrade sealing to exclude moisture (labyrinth seals, V-rings)
- Implement breather or desiccant systems for housings
- Apply protective coating or use stainless steel / ceramic bearings in corrosive environments
