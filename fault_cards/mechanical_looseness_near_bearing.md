# Mechanical looseness near bearing

| Field | Value |
|-------|-------|
| **ID** | BRG-018 |
| **Category** | adjacent_machine_fault |
| **Observability from Vibration** | high |
| **Diagnostic Confidence** | high |
| **Affected Components** | system_level, bearing_seat |
| **Aliases** | bearing-seat looseness | mounting looseness | clearance-driven looseness |

## Symptoms
- Multiple harmonics of 1× RPM (up to 10× or more)
- Sub-harmonics (0.5×) may appear
- Vibration levels vary with load and bolt torque

## Likely Causes
- Loose foundation bolts or hold-down hardware
- Worn or damaged bearing pedestal or housing
- Cracked bearing support structure
- Inadequate grouting or soft foot

## Vibration / Diagnostic Signatures
- Rich harmonic series of shaft speed (1×, 2×, 3×…N×) in radial spectrum
- Possible sub-harmonics at 0.5× RPM and their harmonics

## Confirmatory Tests
- Bolt torque check on bearing housing and foundation
- Structural tap test or operational deflection shape (ODS) analysis
- Strobe light inspection for relative motion at joints

## Recommended Fixes
- Retorque all fasteners to specification
- Replace worn or damaged pedestals and shims
- Repair cracked structures and re-grout foundation
