# Misalignment-induced bearing loading

| Field | Value |
|-------|-------|
| **ID** | BRG-016 |
| **Category** | adjacent_machine_fault |
| **Observability from Vibration** | high |
| **Diagnostic Confidence** | high |
| **Affected Components** | system_level |
| **Aliases** | shaft misalignment | coupling misalignment affecting bearing |

## Symptoms
- Elevated 2× and 3× shaft speed vibration in axial direction
- High axial vibration relative to radial
- Coupling distress or elevated coupling temperature

## Likely Causes
- Angular misalignment between coupled shafts
- Parallel (offset) misalignment
- Thermal growth not accounted for in cold alignment
- Foundation settling or soft foot

## Vibration / Diagnostic Signatures
- Dominant 2× RPM peak in axial spectrum
- 1× and 3× RPM harmonics in axial and radial
- Phase difference of ~180° across coupling in axial direction

## Confirmatory Tests
- Laser alignment measurement of coupling
- Dial indicator or reverse-dial alignment check
- Thermal growth measurement during run-up to operating temperature

## Recommended Fixes
- Perform precision laser alignment to OEM tolerance
- Compensate for thermal growth offsets
- Correct soft foot and foundation issues before alignment
