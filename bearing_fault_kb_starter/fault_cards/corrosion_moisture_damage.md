## Symptoms
- Growing roughness and noise
- Can lead to pitting and later localized defects
- Often linked with poor storage or water ingress

## Likely causes
- Moisture exposure in atmosphere or lubricant
- Poor seals
- Long storage in poor conditions

## Expected vibration signatures
- Early-stage signature is usually non-unique and roughness-dominated
- Later stage may resemble pitting or spalling

## Confirm tests
- Check for water ingress, rust, or lubricant contamination
- Inspect seals and storage conditions
- Look for pitting associated with corrosion

## Fixes
- Eliminate moisture ingress and improve sealing
- Replace contaminated lubricant and damaged bearing
- Improve storage and preservation practice

## Notes
Best treated as a causal/root-cause card plus inspection metadata rather than a pure vibration label.
