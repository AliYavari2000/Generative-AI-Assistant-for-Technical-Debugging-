## Symptoms
- Surface roughness and elevated noise
- Intermittent or persistent high-frequency vibration growth
- Can progress to clearer localized-defect signatures

## Likely causes
- Debris in lubricant
- Moisture exposure
- Poor lubrication

## Expected vibration signatures
- Rough broadband response rather than a perfectly clean single defect family in early stages
- May transition to stronger impulsive behavior as pits enlarge

## Confirm tests
- Inspect lubricant for debris or moisture
- Check seals and environment
- Use envelope plus time waveform trending

## Fixes
- Improve sealing
- Filter lubrication oil thoroughly
- Use proper lubricant
- Replace bearing if pitting is advanced

## Notes
Good card for bridging contamination, corrosion, and localized surface damage.
