## Symptoms
- Premature vibration after recent replacement or installation
- Unexpected early defect growth
- Noise and heat appear sooner than expected

## Likely causes
- Impact during mounting
- Incorrect fits or assembly procedure
- Contamination introduced during installation
- Misalignment introduced during installation

## Expected vibration signatures
- Can mimic localized race/rolling-element defects or looseness
- Pattern depends on the type of damage created during installation

## Confirm tests
- Check maintenance history and time-to-failure after replacement
- Review mounting method and fit tolerances
- Inspect removed bearing for handling marks, dents, or fit-related damage

## Fixes
- Standardize installation procedure and tools
- Control cleanliness and fit tolerances
- Retrain handling practice and replace damaged bearings
